About DangerAlert

DangerAlert is an initiative dedicated to mitigating rape and gender-based violence in South Africa through real-time technology and community support. The system uses verified police data to notify users when they are near locations linked to past incidents. In emergencies, the app triggers a unique loud DangerAlert sound, shares your live location, records evidence, and instantly notifies nearby registered users, chosen contacts, and law enforcement.
